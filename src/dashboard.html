Crop rotation improves soil fertility and prevents pests. For example:

  • After maize, plant beans or groundnuts.
  • After legumes, grow root crops like carrots or potatoes.
  • Rest the soil with cover crops once a year.
This helps reduce chemical fertilizer use and keeps the soil rich.
